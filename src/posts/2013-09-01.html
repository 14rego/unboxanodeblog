This is one of the hardest things for me as a developer. I know there are others who are the same as me, but I also know that it’s not always the “right” thing to do.

This is how it usually plays out: first, I have a need. I need a plug-in. I need a library. I need X. I need Y. Then, I go out and find someone who’s already solved this problem. Ninety-nine percent of the time, someone has already solved this very problem. But here’s my downfall: it’s not good enough.

I like it OK and it’s good enough for now, but later I’m going to come back and “hack the crap out of this” and make it better and mine. Or, at least mine. =o) Some day I’ll make it better, too.

Want to know a secret? … *whispers* … I designed my own PHP MVC framework. Shhhh…
