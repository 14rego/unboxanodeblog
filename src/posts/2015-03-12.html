I’ve been trying to be a little more social about my hobbies. I keep reminding myself that it’s good to meet new people and find new people that I have things on common with.

Some events and groups are more helpful than others, but I’m proud to be a member of KC Women in Technology. While I’m not the best member - nor do I show up to things as often as I should - what this group os doing for women in tech is a great thing.

Maybe you know someone who could benefit? Let us know!
