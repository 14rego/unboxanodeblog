I frequently find that knowing a lot of different languages and processes is to your detriment if you don’t actively engage in practicing at least once a week. It becomes increasingly difficult to remember the small differences over time. I might need to set a reminder to get a ‘refresher course’ every couple of weeks to stay up-to-date. Sad, I know.
