OK, I’m not gonna lie — I’m very wary and skeptical of HTML5, CSS3, and pretty much any jQuery solution. I’ve been practicing jQuery-less code for years, avoiding the odd-job user who, for some reason, turned it off or was using IE6. But it’s time to step up. Webkit is taking over. (THANK YOU, BABY JESUS - AM I RIGHT?!)

These days, I can just insert a little HTML right inline with my text, and I have beautiful, wonderful, responsive icons and symbols. Thank you, Font Awesome! I never thought I would love skipping the whole icon-editing process, but I do. I really, really do.

Now, all I need to do is petition for some more icons I haven’t seen developed, yet…
