New toys are always fun. Restive.js is a fantastic new toy for web developers who are looking for a new tool for their responsive or adaptive styling toolbox.

To cut to the chase, Restive allows you to “sniff out” devices and breakpoints simply and [almost too] easily. Four quick lines of javascript can ease breakpoint headaches web developers have been suffering from for years!

I’ve actually used it on several sites so far and I can’t see myself looking back any time soon. Give it a try for your next project.
