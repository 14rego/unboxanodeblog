A friend of mine, @jeremyalan, always has my best interests in mind. If not for him and his overly complimentary, highly biased, and largely insane confidence in me, I wouldn’t be nearly the “developer” I am today. In fact, I’m not sure that without his efforts, I’d feel comfortable calling myself a developer at all. Regardless…

My point is — he got me a Raspberry Pi! I mean, I’m not sure what level nerd he thinks I am, but I’m 98% sure that the easiest thing for me to do will be to create a case for it to live in… beyond that — lord, help us all. This is going to be an adventure. But don’t worry, I’m up for it. Let the fun begin.
