I’ve been using FancyBox so long that I hadn’t even realized there were better things out there! Then I found Bootbox. I won’t pretend it’s the only or even the best option when it comes to a jQuery lightbox solution, but I will say I love it.

The markup is easy and simple. It has tons of features. Best of all, I don’t feel like I have to worry about it. It works. Every. Time. It’s too easy not to use.
