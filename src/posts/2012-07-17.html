We’re going to add a few infographics to our Softek site, so I decided to go out and see how everyone was feeling about the impact of infographics to date. I’ve seen hundreds of them and I tend to like them, but what do other people think?

I came across this infographic on Google+: Why Infographics Suck.

Not a terrible illustration, by any means, but I don’t understand the point. A lot of people mention they don’t like them because it’s a fancy attempt at displaying boring information in an interesting way. Maybe it is.

Designers think it’s cluttered and just "done all wrong", in terms of colors and fonts. I can understand this argument, but not everyone can come up with the same ideas and designs or the world would be a very boring place.

Of course people want better SEO - they want more people to find their site. So what if it has a lot of graphics! If having a lot of graphics on the web is a problem, someone should probably tell Pinterest, Getty, Shutterstock, iStockPhoto, Picasa, etc.

Just sayin’.
