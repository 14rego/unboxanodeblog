I can’t wait for this event tonight - it’s been so long since I was out and nerdy at the same time.

/edit/ Well, it was cool, but it wasn’t what I expected. I’m not real sure what it is that I expected, so I guess I’ll give it the benefit of the doubt - it was a good learning experience. However, I’m more frustrated than ever about the browser differences and the availability of HTML5.

Adobe has amazing and creative CSS3 [webkit] filters, blends and transforms well on the way. And, of course, all the tools you need to implement your code, easily and quickly. I’m, personally, seriously considering Adobe’s Creative Cloud Services instead of upgrading my Creative Suite ever again. They’ve really thought about what their users need and like and have truly wrapped it up into a nifty package. Also, I had to leave after the first hour, but I bet it was spectacular.

The coolest thing they told me about, although I haven’t looked into it yet, is an HTML- and CSS-driven coding app called Brackets, which you can fork on GitHub and customize to your liking. I’ll be trying that very soon.
