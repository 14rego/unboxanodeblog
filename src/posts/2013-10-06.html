Ever since I started developing, I’ve learned so much. I learn so much every. Single. Day. I know people say this all the time, but I really mean it.

I can design or develop something one day, then come back not even three weeks later and be astounded that I ever wrote code like that or solved a problem that way.

Guess it’s true what they say about progress. Unfortunately, it also causes me to re-write perfectly good code over and over and over.

On a side-note… one of my older mentors used to always quote a saying that went something like, “When you are through changing, you are through.” [ — Bruce Barton ] I always thought there were much better quotes about change, but this still does the trick.
